# Zed Business

Zed Business provides Zed Pro access and administrative tools for teams. Members use hosted AI models and Edit Predictions. Administrators manage feature availability, data flow, and spending through a central dashboard.

## What's included

Members access [hosted AI models](../ai/models.md) and [Edit Predictions](../ai/edit-prediction.md).

For the organization:

- **Enforced data controls:** Administrators set organization-wide AI and data policies in the Data & Privacy dashboard. This includes [Zed Model Provider](./admin-controls.md#hosted-ai-models), [Edit Predictions](./admin-controls.md#edit-predictions), and [feedback settings](./admin-controls.md#agent-thread-feedback). These configurations override individual member preferences.
- **Data privacy:** Zed stores or trains on prompts only after explicit opt-in. [Data sharing](../ai/ai-improvement.md) requires manual enrollment. Administrators [disable sharing](./admin-controls.md#data-sharing) for the entire organization to prevent opt-ins.
- **[Roles and permissions](../roles.md):** Access levels distinguish owners, admins, and members. Specific roles view billing and organization settings.
- **Consolidated billing:** Licenses and AI usage generate [one organization invoice](../ai/billing.md#organization).
